About HopeForChildren

For over 100 years, we've been fighting for children's rights, saving their lives, and helping them fulfill their potential.

Our Mission & Vision

HopeForChildren is a global organization dedicated to improving the lives of children through healthcare, education, and protection programs. We believe every child deserves a healthy start, the opportunity to learn, and protection from harm.

Founded in 1919, we've grown to become a leading children's charity working in over 80 countries. Our vision is a world in which every child attains the right to survival, protection, development and participation.

We work tirelessly to ensure children's voices are heard, their rights are realized, and their needs are met - especially during times of crisis.

About HopeForChildren

For over 100 years, we've been fighting for children's rights and saving children's lives

Our Mission

HopeForChildren is the world's leading independent organization for children. We work in over 80 countries to save children's lives, fight for their rights, and help them fulfill their potential.

Founded in 1919, we respond to major emergencies, deliver innovative development programs, and ensure children's voices are heard through our campaigning to build a better world for and with children.

We believe every child deserves a future. In the United States and around the world, we give children a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm.

Our Approach

Child-Centered

We put children at the center of everything we do. Our programs are designed with their needs, rights, and voices in mind.

Evidence-Based

Our work is grounded in research and evidence. We continuously evaluate our programs to ensure maximum impact.

Partnership-Driven

We work with communities, governments, and local organizations to create sustainable change for children.

Our Emergency Response Approach

Rapid Assessment

Within hours of a crisis, our teams are on the ground assessing needs and coordinating with local partners.

Life-Saving Aid

We provide emergency food, clean water, medical care, and shelter to children and families affected by disasters.

Child Protection

We establish safe spaces for children and work to reunite separated families during emergencies.

Long-Term Recovery

We stay after the immediate crisis to help communities rebuild and become more resilient to future disasters.
